SMS Communications

By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to receive SMS messages from Findixi.

Message types may include:

You can reply STOP at any time to opt out. Reply HELP for assistance.

We do not sell your mobile information to third parties. We may share your information with service providers and carriers solely for the purpose of delivering SMS services and operating the platform.
